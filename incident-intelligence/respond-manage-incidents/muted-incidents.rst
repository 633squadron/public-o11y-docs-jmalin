What is a muted incident?
************************************

Incidents are triggered in a muted state when a service is in maintenance. See :ref:`ii-service-maintenance:`. No responders are notified when a muted incident is triggered. Muted incidents are read-only and can't be acknowledged, resolved, or rejected.
